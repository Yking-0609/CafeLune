About Lune

A Calm Space for Slow Moments

Lune Café & Patisserie was created as a retreat from the rush — a moon-inspired café in Pune where artisanal coffee and handcrafted desserts are served with intention.

Every detail, from our pastries to our atmosphere, is designed to slow you down and let you savour the moment.
